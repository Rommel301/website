Explore the Technology Behind Our Secure Lockers

Channel Relay Module

A channel relay module is a device commonly used in electronics and automation projects to control high-power devices like lights, motors, and other heavy loads through a low-power signal, often from a microcontroller (e.g., Arduino, Raspberry Pi).

ESP32

The ESP32 is a powerful, low-cost microcontroller with integrated Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, designed for a wide range of IoT (Internet of Things) applications.

Membrane Keypad 4x3

This 4x3 Matrix Membrane Keypad has 12 buttons and arranged in a telephone-line 4x3 grid. It's made of a thin, flexible membrane material with an adhesive backing (just remove the paper) so you can attach it to nearly everything.

Solenoid Lock

The solenoid lock denotes a latch for electrical locking and unlocking. It is available in unlocking in the power-on mode type, and locking and keeping in the power-on mode type, which can be used selectively for situations.

Rfid Card Reader

The reader is a device that has one or more antennas that emit radio waves and receive signals back from the RFID tag.
